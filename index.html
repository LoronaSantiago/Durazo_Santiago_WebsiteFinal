Kasama, located in Chicago's East Ukrainian Village, is a culinary dream
created by chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores. The artisanal bakery
offers a variety of freshly baked bread and pastries, while the restaurant
menu features innovative Filipino dishes. The warm ambiance and diverse
menu tell a story of culinary excellence, with classics with a contemporary
twist and new creations. Kasama eagerly awaits your visit and welcomes
you on a memorable gastronomic journey.
